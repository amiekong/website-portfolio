Society for Advancement of Chicanos/Hispanics & Native Americans in Science (SACNAS)

Starting An Organization

I started a SACNAS Chapter at my university during the last year of my undergraduate studies and have been involved ever since. I believe in what it stands for: fostering the success of Chicanos/Hispanics and Native Americans, from college students to professionals, in attaining advanced degrees, careers, and positions of leadership in STEM.  

Facing Adversity

Leading a new executive-board and starting an organization was a difficult challenge but I gained a lot from the experience including patience, teamwork, and communication.
